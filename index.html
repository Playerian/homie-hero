Homie Heros

Hey Homie Hero, I've got a mission for you!
Travel around the bay area to help as many cities with their homeless problems as you can!
You'll face challenges unique to every city and in the end, become the Homie Hero that you were destined to be!
Use arrow keys or AWSD to move the Homie Hero to a different city! Homie Hero can not go to a different city twice!
